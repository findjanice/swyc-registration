GYC Southwest is a regional affiliate of the Generation of Youth for Christ (GYC) located in Southern California.


We're a youth-initiated and-led movement of Seventh-day Adventists from diverse backgrounds, united in a common commitment to lift up Christ and him crucified through bible study, prayer and Christian fellowship.

Our flagship event is the the Southwest Youth Conference (SWYC) held once a year at Pine Springs Ranch during Labor Day weekend.


We also partner with local churches througout the year to hold revival meetings.

If you've never been to one of our conferences or missed an event, most of the messages and testimonies are recorded by Audioverse. These are available for free.
