Lodge


Registration includes attendance to the conference, 3 nights stay at the Pine Springs Ranch Lodge and 8 delicious plant based meals.


Each lodge room houses up to 6 people. Each room has two queen beds as well as two twin trundles, which are kept under the beds. Each lodge room has a private full bathroom. Bed linens and bath towel sets are provided. Lodge rooms do not have air conditioning; however, fans are available.


Unmarried men and women cannot stay in the same lodge room.

Cabin

Registration includes attendance to the conference, 3 nights stay at the Pine Springs Ranch cabin and 8 delicious plant based meals.


The cabins are carpeted, heated and is furnished with 4 sets of bunks and one queen bed.


There are two separate villages for men and women, North and South respectively. Each village has a centralized bathhouse, complete with sinks, toilets, and private showers.


Married couples cannot stay together in the same cabin
